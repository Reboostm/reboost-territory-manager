LOGO SETUP
----------
Save your ReBoost Marketing logo image as:

  public/reboost-logo.png

The app already references /reboost-logo.png in both the login page and the dashboard header.
Once you upload this file to GitHub (in the public/ folder), the logo will appear automatically.

The image should ideally be square (e.g. 256x256 or 512x512 px) or close to it.
A transparent or dark background PNG works best against the dark UI.
